What is Puppy Yoga?

Puppy yoga is a fun activity where people practice yoga while playful puppies move around the room. It combines exercise, relaxation, and time with animals. Many people say it helps reduce stress and makes yoga more enjoyable.

The idea became popular because it allows people to enjoy yoga while also supporting animal shelters. Puppies become comfortable around people, and sometimes participants even adopt them. Many people find the experience both calming and exciting.

Benefits of Puppy Yoga

  • Helps reduce stress and anxiety
  • Improves mood and relaxation
  • Allows puppies to socialize with people
  • Supports animal shelters and adoptions

Common Puppy Yoga Session Example

Activity Time What to expect
Yoga Warm-Up 10 minutes Participants stretch and prepare for the session.
Yoga with Puppies 30 minutes Puppies roam around while people practice yoga poses.
Puppy Playtime 20 minutes Participants interact, play, and take photos with the puppies.

Fun Fact

Puppy yoga first became popular in the early 2010s. It was created to help animal shelters raise awareness and encourage puppy adoption.
