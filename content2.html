Introduction

Puppy yoga is a unique activity where participants practice yoga while playful puppies roam around the room. It combines exercise with relaxation and interaction with animals. Many people attend puppy yoga because it is fun, stress-relieving, and a great way to spend time with animals.

Before attending a class, it is helpful to know what to expect and how to prepare. Being ready will help you have the best possible experience.

Things to Bring or Prepare

  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • A yoga mat (if the studio does not provide one)
  • Water to stay hydrated
  • A positive attitude and willingness to have fun

Typical Puppy Yoga Session Schedule that we have

Activity Approximate Time Description
Check-In 10 minutes Participants arrive, sign in, and prepare their yoga mats.
Yoga Session 30 minutes Yoga exercises are performed while puppies wander around and interact with people.
Puppy Playtime 20 minutes Participants can relax, take photos, and play with the puppies.

Helpful Tip

Puppies may climb on you during poses! This is part of the fun, so stay relaxed and enjoy the moment. Remember that the puppies are learning to socialize with people.
